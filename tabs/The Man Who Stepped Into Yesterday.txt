time, play the last line as follows:

Avenu Malkenu is played here.

After Avenu Malkenu, the reprised version of this song is an abbreviated form of the original. It starts by playing
the main theme once again starting at fret 9 and then rising by 2 frets. However, this time the theme is played only
twice, and so the second repetition ends like the third one in the original song (except that this time, of course,
it's 2 frets lower than the original ending). The conclusion is slightly different:

  B               E
  Slow..    Hold..  Let ring.
