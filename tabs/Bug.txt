by Trey Anastasio and Tom Marshall
Transcription by Jonathan Healey (makisupa@asu.uswest.net)


I've been playing Bug since I got the Tom and Trey Demo Tape.
This version is different from their live one. It requires mucho
sustain. This is how I get the Trey-esque sustain for this song:
Ross Compressor->TS9 (Light Crunch)->Ibanez DMD2000 Delay (.33ms).
The light delay gives each note a searing/sonic sound. It also helps
to sustain the notes.

Problems: I'm having problems trying to figure out the EXACT chords to
         the chorus. Also, I don't know the exact words used in the chorus.
         Someone is saying something ("Don't need it) in harmony with "It
         doesn't matter.". All summer it sounded like "Der-E-ned."
