Okay, axe-slingers, here's my stab at tab for the opening jam of Stash.

The process of writing out tab for the computer takes so long that I got real
lazy towards the end (actually right near the beginning)! I figured that all
that time could be used learning other stuff or just getting away from it all
and lazing in the sun...SO it's kinda skeletal, but I trust that you can figure
it out.

For those of you who didn't see the profile of Trey in the August issue of Guitar
Player (a good read, BTW), he drops a hint as to some of the chords in the song...
"It's a swing tune, basically a II-V-I kind of thing - Gm, Bb, Em7b5, A7b9, and
then Dm". Funny thing, all those chords are in there, but I haven't found a
section with 'em in that order yet.  I play Dm, Bb, Em7b5, A7b9 over the
"maybe so, maybe not" part.

(esp. slides in this song) and listen very carefully to the recording
and try to emulate his phrasing. I tried to include some of the more
crucial slides and such, but I’m way too fucking lazy to tab every
single nuance and subtlety.
