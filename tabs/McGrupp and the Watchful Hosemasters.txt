  I think McGrupp is a really great overview of early Phish.  It combines nice, jazzy lines, (the intro) a bit of prog-rock, (the verses and outro)
some minor key themes that show up in other tunes, a little 7th chord groove, a lot of open-string enhanced chords, and a latin-flavored jam.
This tune also works well on acoustic.  Have fun!
