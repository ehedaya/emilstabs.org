Here are some guidelines for getting the intro right.
A: pick A string, then a fast rake down the other 4, then strum up, up, down.
D9/A: strum up 7 times.
C/A: gently pick the strings individually (same goes for the last A).
E: fast strum up-down-up-down-up-down-up-down.

Sing over A chord: "Divided sky, the wind blows high..."

Repeat Solo 3 twice, and then move into the improvisational part. As a rule
of thumb, play using the same scale as the chord - D major over the D, E
minor dorian (which is basically the same as D major, but with different emphasized notes)
over the Em, A major over the A. The only exception is that Trey switches to G minor pentatonic
for the BbMaj7 chord. Watch out for this as this is important for the feel of the solo.

note on the A string, so you're left with just your index finger on the D-G-B strings.

to get the right feel for this.
