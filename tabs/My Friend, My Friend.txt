I have attempted to get as close as possible to Trey's fingerings and chord voicings
according to live footage, while staying faithful to the album version.
Occasionally Trey omits certain notes that are filled in by the rest of the band.
Wherever I considered these notes important, I have added them in parentheses.

Before making any changes please watch the video at www.youtube.com/watch?v=d18v_8PuUjM .
Thanks to gdoucette1978 for the excellent footage!

Intro Chords:
-------------
Main theme:
|: D G/D D G/D D A (Bm7/A->A7) A G/A F#m/A A7 Bm7 A (falling chords: G6b5, F#m7, Fmaj7#5, A)

This then leads into one of the 3 endings below:

1: A E G C Fm Bbm Abm G (falling single note Bb->A->Ab) C G Bb Eb Abm Dbm Ebm D :|

2: A Bm Dm A B7 E (A/E->E7) E+5 AbmMaj7 Eb Db E Am B A/B B A/B B A/B B G Am Cm G A D :|

3: A Bm Dm A B7 E (A/E->E7) G C Fm Bbm Abm G (w/falling note) C G Bb Eb Abm Dbm Ebm
