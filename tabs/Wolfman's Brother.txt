I finally got around to tabbing my favorite Phish song.  The structure is
pretty loose, but the parts I transcribed are the composed parts from recent
live versions.  The song is basically a funk jam in A# played on the bridge
pickup.  This is not an EXACT transcription, because there is a great deal of
improvising, which makes it all the more fun to play.  Also, to get the timing
just right, it's best to listen to your favorite Phish tape with a kickin'
"Wolfman's Brother."
