The jam is all C# minor (aka Db minor) pentatonic so the CAGED system will get you through like a pro. Just be sure to listen to the timing for that recurring B-C-Db climb. Luckily it's just a chromatic ascent of three half steps so just find where all the Bs are on the fretboard and remember to throw that in when it comes around. Embellish it with some double stops and other tricks and you'll be set.
