Here's my version of the music to Llama. It deviates only slightly from
the previously posted music, mostly in the voicing/extensions of the A
chord. I figured this song out entirely by watching Trey play it, then
confirmed it in my own mind by playing along with PoN repeatedly. The
chord voicings are those used by Trey, as far as I could tell.
